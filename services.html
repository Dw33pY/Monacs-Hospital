Our Services

Services

Comprehensive medical care delivered with expertise, compassion, and cutting-edge technology.

Comprehensive Medical Care

Offering personalized treatment plans for all your healthcare needs. Our general medicine practitioners provide thorough diagnostics and holistic treatment approaches tailored to each patient's unique health profile.

  • Full diagnostic assessments
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Preventive health screenings
  • Chronic disease management

Advanced Surgical Procedures

State-of-the-art surgeries performed by experienced specialists using the latest medical technologies. Our surgical team handles both routine and complex procedures with precision and care.

  • Minimally invasive procedures
  • Modern operating theaters
  • Post-operative rehabilitation
  • Specialist surgical consultations

24/7 Emergency Services

Around-the-clock care for urgent medical situations. Our emergency department is staffed with experienced physicians and equipped with life-saving technology, ready when you need us most.

  • 24/7 physician coverage
  • Rapid triage system
  • Ambulance services
  • Critical care stabilization

Specialized Maternity Care

Compassionate support for mothers throughout pregnancy and delivery. Our maternity wing provides a safe, comfortable environment with modern facilities and experienced obstetricians.

  • Prenatal care & classes
  • Modern delivery suites
  • Postnatal support services
  • Neonatal care unit
Our Programs

Health Initiatives

Going beyond the hospital walls to create lasting health impact in our community.

Community

Community Health Outreach

We organize and execute comprehensive health outreach programs targeting under-served communities in Ongata Rongai. Our team provides free health screenings, consultations, and educational workshops to promote preventive health practices.

Maternity

Enhanced Maternity Services

We expanded our maternity facilities to provide more comprehensive care for expecting mothers. This includes the addition of prenatal classes, improved delivery rooms, and postnatal support services for a holistic experience.

Diabetes

Diabetes Management Initiative

We launched an initiative focused on diabetes awareness and management. This program provides patients with access to regular screenings, personalized care plans, dietary consultations, and educational materials.
