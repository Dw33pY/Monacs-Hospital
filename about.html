Our Mission

Providing Compassionate, Innovative Healthcare

Monacs Hospital Kenya, located in Ongata Rongai, Kajiado County, is committed to delivering exceptional healthcare services. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert medical team ensure comprehensive care across various specialties.

We prioritize patient safety and satisfaction, striving to improve health outcomes with personalized, compassionate attention to every patient's needs. Our commitment extends beyond treatment — we aim to build lasting relationships with our community.

Our Values

Integrity in Medical Services

Compassionate Care

We prioritize empathy and understanding in every interaction. Our team provides personalized care that respects the dignity and needs of each patient.

Excellence in Service

We strive for the highest standards in all our services. Our commitment to excellence ensures top-quality healthcare through continuous improvement and innovation.

Integrity and Trust

Building trust with our patients and community is fundamental. We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all our operations.

Community Focus

Deeply rooted in Ongata Rongai and Kajiado County, we work tirelessly to enhance the health and well-being of our community through outreach and education.

Teamwork & Collaboration

Our success is driven by the collaborative spirit of our diverse team. We foster a supportive environment where every member contributes to exceptional patient care.

Innovation

We embrace modern medical technologies and progressive approaches to ensure our patients receive the most advanced and effective treatments available.

Your Health, Our Priority
